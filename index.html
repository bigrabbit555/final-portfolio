About Me

I'm a junior at Brown University studying computer science. While I generally steer clear from frontend design and development as much as possible, I stepped out of my comfort zone this semester by taking CSCI 1300: UI/UX. The purpose of this page as a culmination of the course is to highlight all I've learned about user interfaces and user experience, and to show off some of the my best work.

I feel that the simplest design is often the best, and am proud of the balance I believe I've struck between simplicity and style. So please feel free to take a look at the projects I've chosen to include below, and one way or another I hope you leave with some new thoughts about design.
